Hi! I'm Steven

Full-Stack Web Developer

I am a full-stack web developer in Houston, Texas. I graduated in 2008 from Oklahoma State University with a degree in Business Administration with a minor in Marketing. I am passionate about design, technology, clean energy, and sustainability and I have spent my career trying to marry my skill set with the passions that drive me. Through my roles within automobile insurance claims, restaurant management, and the solar energy industry, I found my strengths in customer relations, project management, and optimizing processes and workflows.

I have long been interested in technology and its role in our lives - how it can influence tasks, improve communication, and create solutions for everyday problems. An unexpected layoff in the solar energy industry gave me the opportunity to search for a career that would combine my creative interests with my analytical nature while exploring my love of technology. I attended a 16-week immersive coding bootcamp called DigitalCrafts and found I loved the challenges computer programming and software development bring and the idea that at the end of the day I built something. I continue learning and improving my skills and look forward to exploring my passion projects and building great things.
